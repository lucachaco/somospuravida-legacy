¿Qué hacemos?

We Costa Ricans, want to share with humanity our precious way of living and experiencing a pure life. What we call Pura Vida!

Pura Vida is our essence, our soul and our identity. Is what we say when greet. Is what we say when we farewell. Is what we wish to someone to have. Is what we wish to someone to be.

Pura Vida is what we are! Enjoy it, share it and make yourself a Pura Vida day!
